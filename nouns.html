About Noun Information

When you click on a noun card, you'll find details including:

  • Pronunciation: How to say the noun.
  • Gender: The grammatical gender (M: Masculine, F: Feminine, N: Neuter, Pl: Plural Only).
  • Nominative Plural: The plural form of the noun in the basic subject case.
  • Example Sentence: Showing the noun in context.

This selection aims to provide core grammatical information for each noun. Note that some nouns may have irregular plurals or are only used in one form.
