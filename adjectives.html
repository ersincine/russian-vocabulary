About Adjective Information

When you click on an adjective card, you'll find details including:

  • Pronunciation: How to say the masculine form.
  • Other Nominative Forms: Feminine, Neuter, and Plural (all genders).
  • Example Sentences: Demonstrating agreement with masculine, feminine, neuter, and plural nouns. Each example includes literal and natural English translations.

This guide focuses on basic nominative case agreement. Russian adjectives also change for other cases.
